Effective Date: June 16, 2023 Last Updated: July 26, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how C4 Marketing LLC ("C4 Marketing," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards information when you use Workflow Poster (the "Service" or the "App"). If you do not agree with this Policy, please do not use the Service.

We operate the Service for internal use with a single TikTok Business account. If you later authorize additional users, this Policy will govern their use as well.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information you provide to us

  • Account connection data. When you connect a TikTok account, we receive OAuth authorization information (e.g., access tokens, refresh tokens, token expiry, granted scopes) and a TikTok user identifier.
  • Content and metadata. When you prepare or upload videos, we process titles/captions, media files, thumbnails, and timing/visibility settings you configure.
  • Support communications. If you contact us by email, we collect the content of your message and related contact details.

1.2 Information we obtain automatically

  • Usage logs. We may log event timestamps, API requests, status codes, error messages, queue/latency metrics, and file sizes/durations for reliability and security.
  • Device & technical data. When accessing a hosted dashboard, we (or our hosting providers) may collect IP address, browser type, operating system, and referring URLs.

1.3 Information from third parties

  • TikTok. Subject to your authorization, we may receive account basics (e.g., user ID, username, avatar URL) and lists of videos you published or uploaded. Depending on the scopes you grant and API availability, we may also retrieve post status and basic performance metrics.
  • Other services you connect. If you connect external tools (e.g., Google Sheets for logging analytics), we receive the minimum information necessary to write/read the requested data.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive categories of personal information (e.g., government IDs, health or biometric data) through the Service.

Respond to inquiries, provide technical support, and send service or policy updates. 4. Comply with law.
